Turkish Ravioli

Ingredients for the Recipe;

For the dough:

  • 3 cup flour
  • 1 glass of warm water
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon of salt

For the mortar:

  • 250 grams of minced meat
  • 1 medium sized onion
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • /2 teaspoon chili pepper

For the sauce:

  • 2 spoonful butter
  • 2 tablespoons of tomato paste

For the top:

  • 1 bowl of garlic yoghurt
  • 1 teaspoon of mint
  • 1/2 teaspoon sumac
  • 1 teaspoon of chili pepper
